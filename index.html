Donate

We accept donations by using debit, credit, or your PayPal account. All donations online go through PayPal. Just click on the button above that says donate and a new screen will appear. Then click donate on that screen and it will take you to our PayPal. You can make a one time payment or set up a monthly donation. Our EIN #, which is needed to claim your donation on your taxes, is located on the Donate screen, which will appear when once you click donate on this screen.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to raise money to help find a cure for osteosarcoma and other pediatric cancers, award academic scholarships for youth affected by pediatric cancer, and provide funding for families currently going through pediatric cancer journeys.
