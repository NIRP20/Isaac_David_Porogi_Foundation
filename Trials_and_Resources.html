Clinical Trials and Resources

Below are trusted organizations and resources that provide support, guidance, and assistance for families affected by pediatric cancer and the challenges that come with it.

  • Northwest Indiana Cancer Kids Foundation

    One excellent resource for families is the Northwest Indiana Cancer Kids Foundation. They provide guidance, support, and information about ongoing pediatric cancer trials and programs for children and families facing cancer.

  • Dustin's Place

    Dustin's Place is a wonderful organization that focuses on helping people of all ages through the grieving process. Grieving is very difficult, but it helps when you are not alone.

    Dustin's Place has group sessions according to your age, so you are with peers who understand what you are going through. Just click the heading above to check them out.

  • Making A Difference

    Looking for a great place to go through to be a loved one's paid home caregiver? Look no further! Making A Difference has over 10 years' experience and has a very caring staff.

    Just click the heading above to check them out.
